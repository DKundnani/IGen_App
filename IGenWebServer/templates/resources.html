Remember!


There are multiple factors that affect your disease susceptibility that are not captured in your risk score.




Important!


1. Genetic factors: Our genes are a large part of who we are, but they are not everything. Genes expression varies, and this will influence how a trait appears.

2. Environmental factors: Genetics is not your destiny. The environment is also influential in your susceptibility to diseases. Nutrition, exercise, lifestyle choices, and much more effect our health.

3. Study Reliability: Some studies are more reliable and well-established than others. iGen chooses our studies based on many factors that affect reliability, and we always cite our sources when you receive your results. Check out the studies yourself to dive deeper into your results!
